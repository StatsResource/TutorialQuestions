{\bf(a)} $\Pr(\,\overline{\!X} > 55)$ in a group of 100. 
 \item  $\Pr(\,\overline{\!X} < 53)$ in a group of 40.

 \item  The value of $n$ if $\Pr(\,\overline{\!X} < 49) = 0.1$.
